This past week my mom came to visit! We rode many different modes of Public Transit while she was here - the D Line, the Monorail, the 1 Line, the 40, the 44, the 27, the C Line, and the H Line.

Some of our D Line trips were particularly loud! Here's a short video I took while riding Bus #6045 as we came down West Mercer Place while traveling from Lower Queen Anne to Ballard. This seat at the back of the bus clearly has some kind of broken support & shook like crazy! I eventually moved and sat on the seat to quiet it down but it was still pretty loud.
