# Smooth Ride SEA

## The problem

Seattle's city buses shouldn't feel like a roller coaster. Bumpy roads and worn out
suspension make routes noisy and jarring, discouraging riders and stressing
those with mobility issues.

## Our solution

We're going to make some noise about how noisy our buses are & demand that transit
agencies prioritize comfort through better maintenance and fleet upgrades!
A smoother ride means a more reliable and pleasant system for everyone.

## Join us

Follow [our blog](@/blog/_index.md) for progress updates.
Email [hello@SmoothRideSEA.org](mailto:hello@SmoothRideSEA.org) with your video
or audio clips of noisy trips, the bus number, route and the roughest portion
of your ride. Together we can make Seattle's buses smooth and welcoming!
